About Flow

Flow is built to help small businesses stay ahead of cash flow problems. Most businesses do not fail because they are bad businesses. They get caught out because cash arrives late while bills, payroll, and suppliers do not wait.

What Flow does

Flow helps you understand your cash position, identify risk early, and take simple action. That could mean chasing overdue invoices, reviewing upcoming spending, or getting a clearer picture of how long your cash will last.

Why it matters

Cash flow is stressful because it is unpredictable. Flow is designed to make it simpler, clearer, and more actionable. Instead of just showing numbers, the goal is to help businesses know what to do next.

Where this is going

Today, Flow is a working demo. Over time, it can grow into a real cash flow assistant that connects to accounting software, spots overdue invoices automatically, and helps businesses take action faster.
